🤝 About Buddy — Your AI Study Partner

Smarter, faster, and personal — Buddy turns your notes into an intelligent learning system.

🧠 What is Buddy?

Buddy is your private AI-powered tutor and study strategist. It works entirely based on your uploaded study content.

  • 📁 Organize notes from PDFs, DOCX, PPTX, images & scanned materials
  • 💬 Ask deep questions grounded in your own content
  • 📝 Auto-generate quizzes tailored to your topics
  • 📊 View activity logs and progress across sessions

Think of Buddy as:

  • 📚 A centralized note library
  • 🤖 A context-aware AI assistant
  • 🧩 A quiz generator trained on your inputs
  • 📈 A smart dashboard for study tracking

🚀 How is Buddy Different?

Unlike generic AI tools, Buddy is tailored to you.

  • 🗂️ Upload multiple formats: PDFs, DOCX, PPTX, images, handwritten scans
  • 🧾 Advanced parsing: OCR + natural language cleanup = ready-to-query knowledge
  • 🎯 Hyper-relevant answers: No AI hallucinations. Only what's in your material.

Compared to ChatGPT or Gemini: They forget your files — Buddy remembers them all.

🔄 How It Works — The Buddy Flow

  1. Upload study material: Slides, textbooks, notes — any format
  2. Clean text extraction: OCR and parsing engine organize everything
  3. Ask context-aware questions: Get deep answers, no matter the subject
  4. Generate dynamic quizzes: Personalized MCQs, fill-in-the-blanks, or flashcards
  5. Track progress visually: Every action is logged and can be reviewed later
  6. Iterate and evolve: Continuously improve your content and learning path

🔒 Privacy & Control

Your data stays private and under your control. Files are stored locally or in your secure database. Only minimal context is sent to Gemini for processing.

  • 🔐 You control what’s shared with the AI model
  • 🧠 No training happens on your data
  • 🔧 Future upgrades will support encryption and on-device models
